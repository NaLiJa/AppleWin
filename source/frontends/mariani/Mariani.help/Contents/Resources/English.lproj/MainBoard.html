Configuring the Main Board

Mariani can emulate a variety of Apple II compatible computers.

To configure the main board

  1. Open Preferences under the Mariani menu and navigate to the Computer pane.
  2. Click on the option next to the Main Board label.
  3. Select an option from the pop-up list.
  4. Click on the Reboot Emulator button for the selection(s) to take effect.

Major differences in the main boards are listed below:

Apple ][
48K non-autostarting, Integer BASIC, no lower-case, no 80-column, 6502 CPU
Apple ][+
64K autostarting, Applesoft BASIC version of the Apple ][
Apple ][ J-Plus
64K autostarting, Applesoft BASIC, Japanese version of the Apple ][+
Apple //e
128K machine, lower-case, 80-column, 6502 CPU
Enhanced Apple //e
128K machine, lower-case, 80-column, 65C02 CPU
Pravets 82, 8M and 8A
Bulgarian Apple II clones
TK3000 //e
Brazilian //e clone
Base 64A
Taiwanese Apple II clone

In most cases you will probably want to leave it configured as an Enhanced Apple //e.
