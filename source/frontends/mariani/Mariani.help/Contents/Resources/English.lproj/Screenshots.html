Taking Screenshots and Screen Recordings

Mariani can capture screenshots or screen recordings of the running emulation.

To capture a screenshot

  1. Click on the Screenshot button on the right side of the status bar.
  2. Your screenshot would be saved in the folder specified in the General pane of Preferences.

To capture a screen recording

  1. Click on the Screen Recording button on the right side of the status bar.
  2. Your screen recording will begin, and the button will continue to blink, indicating that recording is in progress.
  3. Click on the Screen Recording button again to stop. The recording would be saved in the folder specified in the General pane of Preferences.

Screen recordings do not capture audio at this time.
