The Status Bar

The status bar appears at the bottom of the main window, and provides quick access to some common functions.

Drives

On the left side of the status bar, circle buttons represent individual drives configured for the emulator. The circles can take one of the following appearances:

  1. Drive is empty.
  2. Drive contains a disk image.
  3. Drive contains a write-protected disk image.

Mute Switch

The Mute button can be used to quickly disable all audio output from the emulator. Click on the button again to re-enable.
