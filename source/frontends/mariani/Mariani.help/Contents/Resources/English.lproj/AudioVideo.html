Audio/Video Settings

To control whether to emulate CRT scan lines

Check or uncheck the 50% Scan Lines option. Leaving a gap between scan lines has a more authentic look.

To change the color for custom monochrome video

Click on the color well next to the Color for Custom Monochrome Display option, and select a new color. The selected color would be used when the Display Type is set to Monochrome (Custom).

To change the volume of the built-in speaker

Drag the slider next to Speaker Volume to control the volume of the built-in speaker.

To change the volume of the Mockingboard

Drag the slider next to Mockingboard Volume to control the volume of the built-in speaker. This option is disabled if a Mockingboard is not inserted in slots 4 and 5.
